Certification through Blockchain

Know how blockchain has revolutionized the manageability, security and integrity of educational assets through its powerful encryption capability and distributed data infrastructure.

What is Blockchain

Block chain technology is used to distribute digital information in an encrypted and decentralized fashion. Such data cannot be altered without the agreement of all the hosts of the network thus making it highly secure

True Traceablity

Blockchain technology using its distributed ledger fundamental can potentially remove mission critical glitches in traceability. All participants have access to this distributed ledger and every digital interaction is recorded in a secure and transparent manner which is resistant to tamper.

Verification

In the world of digital transactions, privacy and authenticity of users are of utmost importance. Through its decentralized and impenetrable system, blockchain technology can easily store and manage digital identities which can be utilized not only on national but cross border platforms for verification.

Transparency

Being an open-source technology, blockchain enables complete visibility to authorised users. The public addresses of user holdings and their respective transactions are open for viewing which forms the differentiator in transparency compared to the workings of traditional financial systems.

Removal of fakes

The unalterable and incorruptible nature of blockchain technology helps in counterfeiting certificates nearly impossible. Since every block in the chain is securely encrypted, any data related to the users’ credentials, when distributed in these blocks is virtually tamper-proof.

Track Achievements

Using blockchain, educational authorities can manage and consolidate student’s data allowing then to create a digital trail. These digital transcripts can be shared by students with potential job-offering companies who in turn can tap into the network to verify the student’s achievement history.

Wallet

Users can be rest assured about the safety of their investment which is concealed within highly encrypted blocks. The user has full control on the wallet and can make any business transactions with a level of ease. Armed with the best safe and secure network environment, Wallet stands to be the best in business.
